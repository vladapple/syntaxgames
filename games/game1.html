Ascii's Quest

Ascii's Quest is a 2D Action RPG & Life Sim hybrid about being torn between the demands of adult life and the nostalgia of being a carefree kid. It's a mix of Persona style social gameplay with Kingdom Hearts like action in a 2D world, with influences including Final Fantasy 9 and Dragon Ball.

Mini-game!

Try out one of the mini-games implemented into the main game! 2048 was originally created by Gabriele Cirulli in 2014.
It is our pleasure to let you try it out!
The goal of the game is to combine the numbers and get to the 2048 tile. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!


Controls: use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the tiles.

If you are on mobile, swipe within the playing field to move the tiles.
