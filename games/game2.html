Monster Hunter

As a hunter, you'll take on quests to hunt monsters in a variety of habitats. Take down these monsters and receive materials that you can use to create stronger weapons and armor in order to hunt even more dangerous monsters.

In Monster Hunter, you can enjoy the ultimate hunting experience, using everything at your disposal to hunt monsters in a new world teeming with surprises and excitement.

Features:

  • Hunt with up to 3 friends to take down giant monsters
  • Explore multiple locations and learn more about the local wildlife
  • Hunt down monsters to increase your hunter's gear and experience
