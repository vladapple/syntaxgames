We are Syntax Games!

Founded in 2016 in a garage in Montreal, Canada, Syntax Games is a small game development studio producing big titles.

After the success of their indie hit Ascii's Quest, released in 2018, the expanding studio moved to their current location and began development of their AAA follow-up. In 2021 Monster Hunter released to critical fanfare and garnered many Game Of The Year award nominations.

Four-time winners of IGN's "Is this a real studio?" award, Syntax Games have always prided themselves on building incredible games on a tight deadline and even tighter budget.

SG maintains a small but capable team of developers and is always looking for new artistic and technical talent. Check out our Careers page for more information.

Syntax Games is committed to creating engaging, pro-consumer gaming experiences free from intrusive DRM and predatory monetization.
